## 🧭 The Transition Log

I am documenting my entire journey from optimizing automotive safety systems to designing and piloting robotic systems that explore the final frontier on Earth.

-   🚗 **Origin Sector:** Solid experience in **Automotive Software Engineering** (C++, Python, Embedded Systems).
-   ⚓ **New Vector:** Certified **ROV Pilot Technician**, professionally trained by **Horizon Geosciences**.
-   ✍️ **The Archive:** Built **[SubseaSignals.com](https://subseasignals.com)** as a NextJS + Headless WordPress (Vercel/MilesWeb) technical portfolio to detail the process.
-   🎯 **Next Milestones:** Mastering custom PCB design (KiCad/Altium), deploying AI/ML for underwater vision, and launching the Subsea Signals YouTube channel.
